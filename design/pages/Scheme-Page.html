Scheme Overview

The Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) is a credit-linked subsidy scheme which is a merger of two schemes, namely Prime Minister's Rojgar Yojana (PMRY) and Rural Employment Generation Programme (REGP). The scheme is implemented by Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) functioning as the nodal agency at the national level.

At the state level, the scheme is implemented through State KVIC Directorates, State Khadi and Village Industries Boards (KVIBs), District Industries Centres (DICs) and banks. The maximum cost of the project/unit admissible in manufacturing sector is ₹50 lakhs and in the business/service sector, it is ₹20 lakhs.
